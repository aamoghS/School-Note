Over Wine

Central Theme: The objectification of women and societal expectations.

Woman molded by man's perception. Woman's own reflection vs. how she is perceived by man. Man looking into woman as a possession. Comparison of physical objects (wine, chair) vs. the woman's intangibility. Takeaway: This poem confronts the societal pressure on women to maintain a certain image, often driven by external perceptions.

Family Album

Central Theme: Contrasting idealized love with the real, lived experiences of everyday people.

Societal portrayals vs. actual manifestations of love. "Consumptive Romeos? Juliets Diphtherial" vs. "a [plain] portly, pince-nez'd neighbor bearing roses". Family photographs capturing ordinary moments. Takeaway: The poem emphasizes the value of authentic expressions of love and the transient nature of life, urging readers to appreciate genuine gestures over dramatized versions.

Golden Anniversary

Central Theme: Enduring, transformative love.

From being polar opposites to a state of unified perception. "fire and water" indicating transformation. Dove symbolizing unity and peace. Takeaway: This poem celebrates the journey of love that transcends differences, redefines identities, and creates a bond that withstands the test of time.

"I am Too Close for Him..."

Central Theme: Paradox of intimacy and the complexity of relationships.

Proximity does not always mean deep connection. "flying over him," signaling yearning and distance. "a large house is on fire" representing an unnoticed emotional crisis. Takeaway: The poem delves into the often unnoticed intricacies of relationships, emphasizing that closeness can sometimes lead to invisibility.

"Titanettes"

Central Theme: Shifting beauty standards across eras and the cyclical nature of societal ideals.

Celebrating body diversity against the backdrop of changing societal preferences. "Daughters of the Baroque" vs. "skinny sisters." Heaven populated by "pudgy angels" and a "chubby god." Takeaway: The poem is a celebration of body diversity, highlighting the changing ideals of beauty over time, and urging individuals to recognize the value beyond trends.
